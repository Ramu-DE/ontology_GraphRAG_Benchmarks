📖 KEY FORMULAS:

Cosine Similarity:
  cos(A, B) = (A · B) / (||A|| × ||B||)
  Range: -1 (opposite) to 1 (identical)

Attention:
  Attention(Q, K, V) = softmax(QK^T / √d_k) × V

Temperature Sampling:
  P_i = exp(logit_i / T) / Σ exp(logit_j / T)
  T ↓ = more deterministic
  T ↑ = more random
