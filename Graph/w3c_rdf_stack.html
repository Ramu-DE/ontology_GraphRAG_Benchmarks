What RDF Actually Is

RDF represents information as triples:

Subject
→
Predicate
→
Object
"Customer_99" → "operates_in" → "Region_APac"
"Order_4821" → "belongs_to" → "Customer_99"

Chain enough triples and you get a graph that machines can traverse, reason over, and use as grounded context.
